About Us

Mane Adige (a unit of FFSPL) came into being on 24th of July, 2017 with a soulful purpose of serving home cooked affordable food to meet the cravings of people who dearly miss their native cuisine.

Our motto at Mane Adige is to prepare hygienic affordable and gratifying food keeping in mind the aspects of quality and quantity. Our experienced staffs and cooks are from our own native land who believe in providing a varied menu to satisfy the tastebuds of every individual.
